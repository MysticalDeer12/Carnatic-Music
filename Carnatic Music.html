Carnatic Music

What is carnatic music?

Carnatic music is a south indian style of music. It is widely known around India, especially South India. Carnatic means traditional which means it is usually sung for devotion.

What are some famous instruments used in carnatic music?

The most common instrument used in Carnatic music is VOCAL!! But here are some top 5 famous instruments used in carnatic music!

Top 5 famous carnatic instruments

  • 1. Veena: A stringed instrument that can produce sounds when plucked.

  • 2. Violin: A stringed instrument that can produce sounds when bowed. It has a different posture then normal postures as shown in the picture.

  • 3. Flute: A wind instrument that looks slightly different than a normal flute and is usually seen in Indian concerts.

  • 4. Tabla: A set of twin instruments while one is slightly larger to play a slightly different note. Tabla is usually used to accompany vocal and instrument music!

  • 5. Saxophone: An instrument that is usually played as solo or is used to accompany vocal. They are also famous in many Indian concerts!
